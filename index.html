Oceans are helping us

Oceans play a crucial role in regulating the Earth's climate, absorbing carbon dioxide, and providing food and livelihoods for billions of people.

Oceans are in danger

Overfishing, pollution, and climate change are threatening marine ecosystems, leading to the loss of biodiversity and the degradation of ocean health.
